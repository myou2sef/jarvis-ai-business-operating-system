# JARVIS – AI Business Operating System

Final project for the Building AI course

## Summary

JARVIS is an AI-powered business operating system designed to help organizations manage documents, knowledge, projects, and business processes through intelligent AI agents. The goal is to create a unified workspace where employees can find information, automate routine tasks, and make better decisions using artificial intelligence.

## Background

Many companies use multiple disconnected systems for documents, emails, projects, customer information, and internal knowledge. As organizations grow, information becomes difficult to find and important knowledge is often lost.

This creates several problems:

* Employees spend time searching for information
* Business knowledge is scattered across multiple systems
* Repetitive tasks reduce productivity
* New employees require significant training
* Decision makers do not always have access to complete information

JARVIS aims to solve these problems by acting as a central AI-powered knowledge and business assistant.

## How is it used?

Users interact with JARVIS through a web or desktop application.

Examples of use cases:

* Searching company knowledge using natural language
* Finding documents and project information
* Generating summaries and reports
* Assisting employees with daily tasks
* Organizing project requirements and documentation
* Providing recommendations based on historical data

Potential users include:

* Business owners
* Managers
* Project teams
* Administrative staff
* IT departments

## Data Sources and AI Methods

### Data Sources

The system may use:

* Business documents
* Project files
* Company policies
* Knowledge bases
* Historical project records
* User interactions and feedback

### AI Methods

The system combines multiple AI technologies:

* Natural Language Processing (NLP)
* Large Language Models (LLMs)
* Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
* Document Classification
* Information Extraction
* Recommendation Systems

These methods allow the system to understand documents, answer questions, summarize information, and assist users in decision making.

## Challenges

Several challenges need to be considered:

* Protecting sensitive business data
* Preventing incorrect AI-generated information
* Maintaining data quality
* Managing user permissions and access control
* Ensuring transparency in AI recommendations

Human oversight is required for important business decisions.

## What Next?

Future versions of JARVIS may include:

* Voice interaction
* Multi-agent collaboration
* Predictive analytics
* Workflow automation
* Computer vision for document processing
* Integration with ERP and business systems

The long-term vision is to create a digital business assistant that helps organizations operate more efficiently while keeping humans in control.
